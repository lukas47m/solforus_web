Frequently Asked Questions

Is my private key safe with SolWallet?

Yes. SolWallet never has access to your private keys. They are encrypted and stored only on your device. We recommend always backing up your seed phrase in a secure location.

How does SolWallet keep fees so low?

By communicating directly with the Solana blockchain and only taking a minimal fee on swaps (0.25% via Jupiter API), we can offer significantly lower fees than competitors who charge 0.8% or more.

Can I use SolWallet for all types of Solana tokens?

Yes. SolWallet supports all tokens built on the Solana blockchain, including SPL tokens and NFTs. You can create token accounts for any valid token on the network.

How do I back up my wallet?

When creating a new wallet, you'll be given a 12 or 24-word seed phrase. Write this down and store it in a secure location. With this seed phrase, you can recover your wallet on any device.

What makes SolWallet different from other Solana wallets?

SolWallet focuses on direct blockchain interaction with minimal third-party involvement. We're building a tool that enables full utilization of the Solana ecosystem, not just an investment platform. Our low 0.25% swap fee and growing smart contract integrations set us apart.
